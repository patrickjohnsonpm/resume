Professional Summary

I am a passionate and hard working mathematics teacher who strives to impart deep mathematical knowledge and understanding to my students. My core values include a desire to give all students the chance to achieve to the best of their abilities by providing support to students who struggle while also providing opportunities for more advanced students to strive for excellence. I believe education to be one of the strongest forces to promote social justice and provide life changing opportunities to students in our societies, and as such feel privileged to have been involved in the profession.

After teaching in a wide variety of contexts over my ten years as a teacher, I moved into the private sector, working as a data analyst using cutting edge tools over the past seven years. I believe applying my deep knowledge in mathematics to real world industry problems enables me to impart a sense of importance on the subject material that I deliver. Encouraging the use of new technologies in the teaching of mathematics is fundamental to preparing the next generation of students in technical fields.
